CHAOS AT ITS FINEST

Homigrad combines the best from Garry's mod: gunplay, physics, and player interaction, to create one of the funniest and most chaotic gamemodes in the community. Imagine TTT on crack!

SO, SO MANY FEATURES

RAGDOLL CLIMBING

Scale up buildings, grab, tackle, and drag people, grab onto objects, and even shoot while climbing or mid-air, Max Payne style!

MEDICAL SYSTEM

Getting hurt has consequences. You can bleed out, get knocked out, and die if you don't banadage wounds or get blood bags. CPR can be used to revive people, but it's not always successful.

GORE

Getting shot can rip off limbs, and falling from a high place can explode you on impact. Losing a limb isn't a death sentence, but it can be a major disadvantage.

CUSTOM WEAPONS

Utilize our arsenal of 40+ firearms and 20+ melee weapons to wreak havoc on other players. In our upcoming update, experience completely custom animated weapons with thirdperson animations, customization, and gun skins!

CLASSES

Our gamemodes have classes! We've created more than a dozen custom classes, each with their own unique equipment.

HOMIGRAD'S BREAD AND BUTTER: GAMEMODES

Gamemodes are core to Homigrad. We've created more than a dozen custom gamemodes, each with their own unique mechanics and features.

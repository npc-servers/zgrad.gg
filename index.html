RAGDOLL CLIMBING
Experience unprecedented realism with our advanced ragdoll physics system. Climb over fallen bodies, use them as cover, and interact with the environment in ways never before possible in TTT.

Advanced medical mechanics add depth to survival gameplay. Treat wounds, perform CPR, use medical equipment, and manage health conditions that affect your performance and strategy.
GORE
Realistic gore and damage effects create intense, immersive combat. Visual feedback from injuries adds weight to every encounter and enhances the brutal atmosphere of TTT.

Choose from specialized classes each with unique abilities and equipment. Whether you prefer stealth, assault, support, or reconnaissance, each class offers distinct gameplay mechanics and strategic advantages.

HOW TO APPEAL A BAN

Learn how to properly appeal your ban and get back to playing on ZGRAD servers

Back to Home

Uh oh, it looks like you're banned! But don't worry, you can submit an appeal to get it lifted or reduced.

Head to Discord

Join our Discord server: discord.gg/npc

Go to the Help Channel

Click here: Direct Link to #help Channel

Or navigate to the #help channel manually.

Open a Ban Appeal Ticket

Find the ZGRAD Support category, click the dropdown, and select "Appeal".

Fill Out the Information

Complete the form with information about your ban.

Need your SteamID? If you're unsure of how to get your SteamID, go to https://steamid.gg and paste your Steam profile URL, then select SteamID32.

Wait for Response

Check the pinged channel and wait for a response to your ticket. Our staff will review your appeal and get back to you!

New to Homigrad? This guide covers what Homigrad is, how to get started, important controls, and tips when playing.

What is Homigrad?

Homigrad is a chaotic multi-gamemode, round based experience where players are given a team or role, and must survive the round or complete their objective to win.

Basic Controls

Common controls you'll use:

  • G - Ragdoll
  • H - Drop held item
  • (while ragdolled) Left/Right mouse button - Extend/move arms
  • (while ragdolled) Q/E - Grab with hands
  • V - Kick

Role-specific actions will be explained in-game or in role descriptions; pay attention to the beginning of the round!

Tips for New Players

  • You can press F2 to access our in-game settings menu to customize your experience and change your keybinds.
  • Text and voice chat is in a proximity.
  • When a player dies, you can open their inventory and steal their items.
  • Certain injuries can hinder your movement or actions.

Troubleshooting

Having trouble connecting or with gameplay?

  • Ensure you have the setting "Allow all custom files from server" on in your Options under "When a game server tries to download custom content to your computer".
  • Check our connect page for server addresses and instructions.
  • If you encounter a bug, open a report forum on our Discord.

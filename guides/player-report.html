HOW TO MAKE REPORTS

Learn how to properly report rule violations and problematic players on ZGRAD servers

Back to Home

Encountered a rule breaker or cheater? Here's how to report them so our staff can take action!

Head to Discord

Join our Discord server: discord.gg/npc

Go to the Help Channel

Click here: Direct Link to #help Channel

Or navigate to the #help channel manually.

Open a Player Report Ticket

Find the ZGRAD Support category, click the dropdown, and select "Report Player".

Copy the Person's SteamID

In-game, left-click on the player's name in the scoreboard and click the "Copy SteamID" button. You'll need this for the report.

Fill Out the Information

Complete the form with details about the rule violation. Include screenshots or videos if you have them!

Evidence Upload: If you can't upload evidence to a hosting site like Medal.tv, Dropbox, etc., you can paste it directly in the ticket chat after it's created.

Wait for Review

Check the pinged channel and wait for a response to your ticket. Our staff will investigate and take appropriate action!

Need immediate help? If someone is actively breaking rules, you can type @ (your message, no parenthesis) in-game to get staff attention quickly. Beware, only IN-GAME staff will see this!

Map Requirements

Spawn Points

  • At least 42 info_player_counterterrorist spawns
  • At least 42 info_player_terrorist spawns
  • CT and T spawns must be separated from each other as team spawns
  • You can add any number of info_player_start spawns located anywhere on the map

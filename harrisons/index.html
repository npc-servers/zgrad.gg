HARRISONS HOMIGRAD IS NOW ZGRAD

Harrisons Homigrad merged with the ZGRAD network in early January 2025.

Why We Merged

When the original founder of Harrisons Homigrad faced challenges in maintaining server infrastructure, ZGRAD stepped forward to ensure the community's legacy would continue. This merger isn't just about server hosting - it's about evolution. ZGRAD is fully committed to:

  • Providing enhanced server hardware for better performance
  • Regular updates and improvements to the gamemode
  • Maintaining the core experience players love
  • Expanding features and community capabilities

By joining forces, we're ensuring that the spirit of Harrisons Homigrad lives on while gaining the resources and infrastructure to grow even further.

Who We Are

We are a dedicated team of gaming enthusiasts who have been running successful Garry's Mod communities. The ZGRAD network brings together the best aspects of both communities, maintaining the unique characteristics that made each special while creating something even greater. Our combined experience and resources allow us to deliver an unparalleled gaming experience.
